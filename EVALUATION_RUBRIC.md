# Evaluation Rubric

We will evaluate submissions across the following dimensions.

## Problem Structuring
- Clear understanding of onboarding workflow
- Logical decomposition of the process
- Good judgment around priorities and edge cases

## AI Application Quality
- Practical and relevant use of AI
- Good handling of document processing and classification
- Thoughtful use of prompts and decision logic
- Appropriate personalization or recommendations

## Automation Design
- Strong workflow structure
- Sensible integrations
- Good handling of handoffs and data movement
- Awareness of failure points and human review steps

## Demo Quality
- Working scaffold or convincing prototype
- Clear representation of the automation logic
- Organized files and documentation
- Easy to review

## Communication
- Clarity
- Brevity where needed
- Strong documentation
- Professional presentation

## Bonus Points
- Security and compliance considerations
- Auditability and logging
- Error handling
- Scalability considerations
- Good UX thinking for HR and new hires
