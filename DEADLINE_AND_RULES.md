# Deadline and Rules

## Time Expectation
This exercise is designed to take approximately 1 to 2 days.

## Rules
- You may use AI tools, automation platforms, and external references
- You may use mock data and placeholders where appropriate

- Focus on clarity, practicality, and workflow design
- Your demo only needs to show the core automation logic

## Submission Deadline
Please submit your pull request with in next 2 days late than 48 hours of sharing the assessment with you, your assessment will be considered as disqualified.
